## 介紹

# Domra

The domra (Russian: домра, Russian pronunciation: [ˈdɔmrɑ], pl. домры, domry) is a long-necked Russian folk string instrument of the lute family. It has a round body and three or four metal strings.

## 音色描述

音色由弦長、張力、共鳴箱與演奏法影響，可呈現撥奏、擦奏或擊弦的不同質感。
